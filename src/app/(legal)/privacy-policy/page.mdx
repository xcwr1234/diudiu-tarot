2. **Tarot Reading Data**

3. **Usage Details**

   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use our tarot services, including your interactions with different decks, spread types accessed, and reading frequency.

4. **Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access ShipAny, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our tarot reading interface for different devices and ensure compatibility.

5. **Cookies**

   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our tarot reading services and personalize your spiritual journey.

6. **Service Usage Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information about your tarot reading preferences and service usage patterns.
   - **Purpose**: To improve our tarot reading services and provide better spiritual guidance.
